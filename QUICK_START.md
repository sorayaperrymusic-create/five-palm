# FIVE Palm Jumeirah - 7-Day TikTok Storyboard Presentation

## Quick Start Guide

### For Viewers (People You Share With)

Simply click the link and the presentation will open in your browser. It auto-plays with music!

**Controls:**
- **Play/Pause** button at the bottom
- **← →** buttons to navigate slides
- **Keyboard:** Arrow keys, Space to play/pause, M to mute

### For Deployers (You)

Follow the steps in `GITHUB_RAILWAY_SETUP.md` to deploy to Railway.

## What's Included

- 12 professionally designed slides
- 7 full-length Soraya Perry music tracks
- Auto-play functionality
- Responsive design (works on all devices)
- Keyboard and button controls
